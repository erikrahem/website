Getting started
Pixel art (2D) Use Aseprite Practice... Try to redraw Pokémon sprites. First maybe pixel by pixel, then as you learn try to predict the next pixels by guessing or without looking at the original image twice. Draw the same Pokémon from different games and ultimately you should be able to draw the Pokémon recognizably in your own style and way.
