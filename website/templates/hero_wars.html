Hero Wars

An RTS game project idea in fantasy medieval setting, divided into game components.
Inspired by Dawn of War I & II. Current plan draft: use intercompatible graphics from Synty Studios' asset packs. The main idea is to be able to bring your hero and develop him in different game modes. A huge challenge would be the balancing and detail tweaking, everything must work perfectly together, from the speed and pace of everything, to health and combat systems, etc. This project requires a lot of effort and brains.

Hero Builder (I)

The joy of customization.
Like in 'Dawn of War II - Last Stand' and the campaign of 'Dawn of War I' I want to offer hero development options. Development: using this asset pack, endless variations of characters and equipment/items can be made. With this, spells/abilities can be easily designed.

Assassinate (II)

Simple and fun.
With these asset packs, maps can be easily made for simple battle modes between heroes.

Last Stand (III)

Fun.
This asset packs provides 20 different monsters, that can be used in this case for waves in a survival arena game mode, where you and your team mates' must persevere. These creatures must also have their spells/abilities designed though.

Knight Wars (IV)

RTS.
The ultimate destination for heroes, where they can fight with big armies of knights, and the like, in traditional RTS settings. Maybe factions can be added, and feature (some of) the monsters from Last Stand. More possibilities include making more types of human units available, or perhaps allow more than one hero from a player's account. Standard knights could be randomly generated using this pack. This pack can be used to make more human army units, like sorcerers for example.
